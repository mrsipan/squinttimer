⏳ Visual Timer for Kids

A countdown timer that shows time passing — perfect for little ones who can't read clocks yet!

✨ No reading required  •  Just watch the red disc shrink

What is it?

Imagine a bright red circle that slowly gets smaller clockwise — like a pizza being eaten, but for time! Kids instantly see how much time is left. Simple, friendly, and fun.

👆

Drag to set time

Just drag the slider or tap the numbers — up to 30 minutes. No complicated menus.

🕒

Big MM:SS display

Large, clear numbers so even grown-ups can read it from across the room.

⏯️

Start / Pause / Reset

Easy buttons with friendly icons. Pause for snack breaks, reset to start over.

🔔

Beep at zero

A cheerful beep sounds when time's up — no shouting needed!

⏱️

Max 30 minutes

Perfect for toothbrushing, reading, playtime, or a short nap.

🌐

Works in browser

No install! Open the page on any phone, tablet, or computer — it just works.
